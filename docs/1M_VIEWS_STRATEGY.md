# 1M Views Strategy (Autonomous Execution Ready)

## Objective
Reach 1M views using:
- high volume
- massive A/B testing
- emotional triggers

---

## Core Formula

Views = Volume x Hit Rate

No guaranteed viral.
Scale increases probability.

---

## Operational Targets

- 50–100 videos/day
- 5–10 accounts
- 5 variations per video

→ 250–500 variations/day

---

## Video Structure

### Hook (0–2s)
Critical.

Use patterns:
- This might be happening to you...
- Nobody talks about this...
- If you feel this, pay attention...
- This explains what you feel...
- You don’t notice this but it affects you...

Fail here = video dies

---

### Body (3–10s)

Rules:
- 1 idea
- 1 emotion
- no filler

Example:
"You rest... but still feel tired"

---

### Ending

Soft CTA:
- Save this for later
- This might help you someday

---

## Formats

- Micro reflection
- Dual voice
- Mental loop
- Contrast

---

## A/B Testing

Each idea:
- 5 hooks
- 2 captions
- 2 edit styles

→ 10 versions

---

## Distribution

Post on:
- TikTok
- Instagram
- YouTube Shorts

---

## Scaling Rule

If video performs:
- repost 2–3 times
- tweak hook/caption/edit

---

## Metrics

Focus:
- Retention > 30%
- Shares
- Comments

---

## Decision Rule

- 2–4h no traction → discard
- good performance → scale

---

## Execution Mode

System must run:
- 24/7
- autonomous
- no manual input

---

## Integration Flow

Trend → Emotion → Content → Variations → Distribution → Optimization
